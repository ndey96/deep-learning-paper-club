Deep Learning Reading Club

Understanding a research paper can be a time consuming endeavour. With the massive output of deep learning research it can be difficult to keep up with all of the most exciting ideas. The purpose of this club is help students understand and discuss exciting deep learning papers in a collaborative setting.
